  A reproducible closed-loop agent that **imagines the next second of gameplay** with a video world model and decodes it into controls — no hand-written policy, no scripted primitives. Each cycle: real frame → VLM caption → WAN-2.2 imagines motion → VPT IDM decodes keyboard/mouse → MineStudio executes. Dropped into `extreme_hills` with nothing pre-spawned; the agent explores, finds exposed stone, and mines it (seed 99, first mine at cycle 23).
